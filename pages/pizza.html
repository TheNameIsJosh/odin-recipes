Pizza Recipe

Description

This is a pizza recipe for tasty, homemade pizza. Cheezy and savory, this recipe is sure to make your palette satisfied with a pie that is easy to make, prepare, and eat. The pizza is easy to make to flavour, and this recipe will demonstrate a pizza with pepperoni

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water (110 degrees F/45 C)
  • 0.25 ounce of active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons of oilve oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients, preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 C), and lightly grease pan.
  2. Put warm water in a bowl, add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to mixture, then beat until smooth. You can do so by hand or with a stand mixer with a dough hook.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Transfer pizza to the pizza pan.
  7. Spead crust with sauce and toppings of your choosing.
  8. Beake in the oven until golden-brown (15-20 minutes). Remove from oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
