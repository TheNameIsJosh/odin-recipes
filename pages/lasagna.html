Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a very popular, easy-to-make lasagna recipe. Meat sauce, noodles, and cheezy ricotta layered on top of each other with slices of mozzarella and a sprinkle of parmesan make this dish a cheezy, flavorful plate. The lasagna is easy to add things to, so you can always alter it to taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, divided or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven (medium heat) until well browned.
  3. Stir in tomato ingredients and water. Add sugar, 2 tablesoons of parsley, basil, 1 tablespoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer while covered for 1 1/2 hours, stir occassionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain them and rinse with cold water
  5. In another bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg, the remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoons of salt.
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 C).
  7. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Cover with foil (to prevent sticking, spray the foil with cooking spray or make sure it does not touch the cheese).
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
