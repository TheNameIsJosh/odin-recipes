Rice and Fish Recipe

Description

This is a very popular, easy-to-make lasagna recipe. The lasagna is easy to add things to, so you can always alter it to taste. This specific recipe is the base lasagna which you can use to base your own off from.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of salt, divided or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather Ingredients.
  2. cook sausage, ground beef, onion and garlic in a dutch oven (medium heat) until well browned.
  3. Stir in tomato ingredients and water. Add sugar, 2 tablesoons of parsley, basil, 1 tablespoon salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer while covered for 1 1/2 hours, stir occassionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a broil.
  5. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Transfer pizza to the pizza pan.
  7. Spead crust with sauce and toppings of your choosing.
  8. Bake in the oven until golden-brown (15-20 minutes). Remove from oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water (110 degrees F/45 C)
  • 0.25 ounce of active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of white sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups of bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons of oilve oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients, preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 C), and lightly grease pan.
  2. Put warm water in a bowl, add yeast and sugar. Mix and let it stand for about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to mixture, then beat until smooth. You can do so by hand or with a stand mixer that has a dough hook.
  4. Let it rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough onto lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Transfer pizza to the pizza pan.
  7. Spead crust with sauce and toppings of your choosing.
  8. Bake in the oven until golden-brown (15-20 minutes). Remove from oven and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
